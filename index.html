Artist of the day:
Josiah Brooks

Josiah Brooks, or Jazza, is an Illustrator and Youtuber, who started working from his garage with a perspective of making his dream come true. He teaches and inspires his 3.2 million fans and instructs them how to draw and vivify. Australian local Josiah has put his hands in numerous areas, and he is well known as a YouTuber, business visionary, and a family man. He is outstanding for his YouTube channel ‘Draw with Jazza’ where he instructs how to paint, energize and draw.
